Website Designer/Data Miner (two co-op work terms)

- Enablers.ca, The Disability Site Association

I designed this website that serves the needs of people with disabilities. I used Drupal 7 CMS and Google Analytics for the development. I installed more than 50 modules to make it a user friendly website especially for people having visibility and hearing problems. I made the website form non-functional to functional.
I was also featured in the Chronicle Herald Newspaper. The website is a quick-handy tool to find helpful information such as - Understanding your disability, diet and exercise, mobile apps for diseases, finding a local healthcare professional, government and financial support - basically everything you need to know. The website has more than 4000 webpages worth of useful information.
I'm still attached with this not-for-profit organization, and I tend to work on the website (volunteer) during the weekends.
